description:
The Product Landing Page project is a promotional site for 
"Tolo Fast Food," featuring a clean and modern design.
 It includes sections for menu items, contact information,
 and a special offer for free home delivery. 
Users can browse through various food options like pizza, kabab, 
and burgers, and access contact details for placing orders. 
This project showcases skills in HTML, CSS, 
and responsive web design, emphasizing user-friendly
 navigation and a visually appealing layout.
